US Cases of COVID-19

Created by students of Rutger's Data Science BootCamp. Our team, COVIDgilance, is made up of Paul Kazalski, Flomo Kokro, Will Giordano, Maria-Elena Gorini, Meghna Agnish. Our goal was to create visualizations showing the growth of the number of COVID-19 cases in the US as well as the total number of deaths from COVID-19 in the US. Resources used included Mapbox, Leaflet, and a slider function courtesy of Sol Vitkin, and https://www.worldometers.info/coronavirus/
